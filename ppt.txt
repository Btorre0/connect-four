Connect 4
Beatriz Torres,
Jake EstradaOverview
A classic Connect Four implementation built with React and
TypeScript, featuring an interactive game board where a human
player competes against an AI opponent powered by minimax
algorithm with alpha-beta pruning to connect four pieces in a
row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.3
Problem Formulation (State-Space Representation)
●State Representation: The game state is represented as a 6×7 grid Board, where
each cell can be empty, occupied by the human player, or occupied by the AI.
●Actions/Operators: Valid actions are dropping a piece into any of the 7 columns
that are not full. The dropPiece function places the piece in the lowest available
row of the selected column and generating a new board state.
●Goal State: A terminal state is reached when either player achieves four
consecutive pieces horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, or when the board is
completely filled resulting in a draw.4
AI Technique Used
●Minimax algorithm
○ The AI evaluates future moves to decide the best
●Depth-limited search
○ Rather than just thinking about the next move, it thinks multiple turns
ahead.
●Alpha-beta pruning
○ Removes unnecessary branches and speed up calculation
●Heuristic evaluation to scoreboard states
○ Prefers center control + 3-in-a-row setups
○ Blocks opponent winning moves
○ Detects whether or not it’s winning or losing
